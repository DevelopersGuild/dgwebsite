Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't have a programming background. Can I join?

A: Yes! There are many things that we need help with, including user interface design, artwork, and team management. Of course, any programming experience you do have will help you communicate with the team.

Q: Am I too advanced for this club?

A: You might be more technically advanced, but what we offer are teams with a purpose.

Q: How involved do I need to be?

A: That all depends on how much you already know, most members will have to learn on their own time to keep up, but we'll make sure you aren't wasting time learning things that aren't important!

Q: How can I find people work on my Guild project?

A: Come to one of our meetings and ask the officers, that's the quickest way!

Q: I don't have any ideas for my own projects. Or, I don't want to make a new project. Is that okay?

A: Of course it is! Don't feel pressured to do something you don't want to, we are here to help you enjoy becoming one with the code!

Q: Do I have to be a De Anza College student to join?

A: Yes. We are an incorporated De Anza College club, and we may only accept local students. It is in our constitution and will remain this way unless new circumstances arise.

Q: What projects can I build?

A: You can build websites, mobile applications, games, whatever you want! We are here to support our member's ambitions!/p>

Q: I don't have any programming experience. Will you help me learn?

A: We won't tutor you ourselves, but we can give you many resources that other students have used to successfully learn basic programming. Also, being around other programmers more skilled than yourself will help you to grow in your programming abilities.

Q: How often do you meet?

A: Every week. We can are located in room AT___ at 1:30PM!

Q: I don't have a laptop. Is that okay?

A: It depends on what project you wish to work on. We can provide you with school desktop computers that run Windows. But some projects, for example iOS, cannot be developed on Windows computers.

Q: Is there a fee for joining?

A: No. Developers' Guild is free for all De Anza College students and will always remain that way. We are here to help the students of De Anza embrace technology through creation of their ideas!
