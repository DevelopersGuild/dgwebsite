Frequently Asked Questions

Q: I don't have a programming background. Can I join?

A: Yes! There are many things that we need help with, including user interface design, artwork, and team management. Of course, any programming experience you do have will help you communicate with the team.

Q: Am I too advanced for this club?

A: Possibly!

Q: How can I find people work on my project?

A: Come to one of our meetings and ask us!

Q: I don't have any ideas for my own projects. Or, I don't want to make a new project. Is that okay?

A: Of course.

Q: Do I have to be a De Anza College student to join?

A: Yes. We are an incorporated De Anza College club, and we may only accept local students.

Q: What projects can I build?

A: TODO

Q: I don't have any programming experience. Will you help me learn?

A: Not ourselves, but we can give you many resources that other students have used to successfully learn basic programming. Also, being around other programmers more skilled than yourself will help you to grow in your programming abilities.

Q: How often do you meet?

A: Every week.

Q: I don't have a laptop. Is that okay?

A: It depends on what project you wish to work on. We can provide you with school desktop computers that run Windows. But some projects, for example iOS, cannot be developed on Windows computers.

Q: Is there a fee for joining?

A: No. Developers' Guild is free for all De Anza College students.
